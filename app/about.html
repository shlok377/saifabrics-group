About Sai Fabrics Group

A Decade of Precision, Innovation and Trust

Sai Fabrics Group is a leading digital textile printing company based in Surat, Gujarat—the textile capital of India. For more than a decade, we have been serving garment manufacturers, fashion brands, exporters, and textile businesses with premium digital printing solutions that combine creativity, technology, and manufacturing excellence.

Our expertise extends beyond printing. We understand the complexities of textile production, the importance of colour consistency, the value of timely delivery, and the expectations of businesses operating in highly competitive markets. Every metre of fabric we produce reflects our commitment to craftsmanship, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To become one of India's most trusted and preferred digital textile printing partners, recognized globally for innovation, precision manufacturing, and dependable customer relationships.

Our Mission

To empower fashion businesses with world-class digital textile printing solutions through advanced technology, superior quality standards, and a customer-centric approach that creates lasting value.

Why We Exist

Fashion is constantly evolving. Consumer expectations continue to rise. Brands require manufacturing partners who can deliver speed, consistency, creativity, and reliability simultaneously.

Sai Fabrics Group exists to bridge this gap by providing dependable textile printing solutions that help businesses innovate confidently and manufacture efficiently.

For over a decade, businesses have chosen Sai Fabrics Group because we consistently deliver more than printed fabric—we deliver confidence. Our manufacturing processes are designed to ensure consistency across production batches, minimize quality variations, and support our customers in maintaining their own brand reputation.
